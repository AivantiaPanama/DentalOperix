# Governance Architecture Review Board Charter

## Mission

Ensure controlled evolution of the Governance Platform while preserving SDK stability, baseline compatibility, governance standard integrity, and decision traceability.

## Scope

The GARB governs:

- Governance SDK.
- Governance Validation Engine.
- Governance Automation.
- Governance Platform ADR.
- Governance standards.
- Canonical governance models.
- Governance Platform versioning.

The GARB does not govern functional domains such as Leads, Patients, Appointments, Calendar, Gmail, or runtime persistence.

## Decision Outcomes

- APPROVE.
- APPROVE WITH CONDITIONS.
- REQUEST CHANGES.
- REJECT.

## Approval Criteria

Every governance platform change must demonstrate baseline compatibility, no functional architecture impact, public contract compatibility, ADR alignment when applicable, and sufficient audit evidence.
