## Purpose

This document is the master roadmap for turning DentalOperix from a booking, leads, and analytics product into an Intelligent Operating System for Dental Clinics. It is the executive reference for what to build, what can be sold, what remains incomplete, and how to prioritize the next implementation phases.

## Certified Baseline

DentalOperix must continue to respect the certified 57.x persistence architecture:

```text
Leads
  -> LeadPersistencePort
  -> LeadPersistenceProvider
  -> RelationalLeadPersistenceAdapter
  -> Supabase PostgreSQL
```

The permanent commercial source-of-truth rule remains:

```text
Leads = Source of Truth
```

61.x does not reopen 57.x and does not replace Leads. It introduces product governance, identity, role-based dashboards, patient management, and controlled AI-assisted delivery on top of the certified baseline.

## Product Vision

DentalOperix is an intelligent clinical-commercial operating system for dental clinics.

Target operating loop:

```text
Lead
  -> Appointment
  -> Patient
  -> Attendance
  -> Treatment
  -> Revenue
  -> Retention
  -> Analytics
  -> Clinical Intelligence
```

## Commercial Releases

### DentalOperix Starter

Goal: Sellable early product for clinics that need lead capture, appointment management, and basic operational visibility.

Modules:

* Leads and appointment capture.
* Supabase relational persistence.
* Calendar integration.
* Patient confirmation email with ICS.
* Clinic operational notification.
* Assistant Operations Dashboard.
* Admin Starter Dashboard.
* Users and basic RBAC.

Priority: P0
Estimated current completion: 55% to 65%
Estimated remaining effort: 4 to 8 effective weeks
Commercial value: Very high

### DentalOperix Professional

Goal: Operate the clinic beyond lead capture.

Modules:

* Patient Management.
* Doctor Dashboard.
* Appointment lifecycle management.
* Patient contact, email, phone, billing profiles.
* Attendance and no-show tracking.
* Data quality classification.

Priority: P1
Estimated remaining effort after Starter: 8 to 12 effective weeks
Commercial value: High

### DentalOperix Business

Goal: Provide management intelligence and automation.

Modules:

* Advanced Executive Dashboard.
* Funnel analytics.
* Appointment lifecycle analytics.
* No-show analytics.
* Clinical Intelligence MVP.
* AI assistant workflows.

Priority: P2
Estimated remaining effort after Professional: 3 to 6 months
Commercial value: Medium/High

### DentalOperix Enterprise

Goal: Scale to multiple clinics, doctors, and advanced integrations.

Modules:

* Multi-clinic operations.
* Multi-doctor scheduling and capacity.
* Advanced permissions.
* Advanced audit and compliance.
* Integrations with finance, billing, inventory, and external systems.

Priority: P3
Estimated remaining effort: 6 to 12 months after Business foundations
Commercial value: Future/high for larger customers
