# 61.0 Product Release Plan

Status: ACTIVE
Owner: Product Governance
Last updated: 2026-06-20

## Purpose

Define commercial release targets so DentalOperix can be sold before the entire long-term product is complete.

## Release Strategy

DentalOperix should be sold in progressive tiers:

1. Starter: immediate clinic operations and lead conversion.
2. Professional: patient and doctor operations.
3. Business: analytics and operational intelligence.
4. Enterprise: multi-clinic and advanced integration.

## Starter Release

### Customer Promise

DentalOperix Starter helps a dental clinic capture patients, schedule appointments, notify patients, and manage daily follow-up from one operational dashboard.

### Required Modules

- Public Booking.
- Supabase Lead persistence.
- Calendar integration.
- Patient confirmation email + ICS.
- Clinic operational notification.
- Users.
- RBAC.
- Assistant Dashboard.
- Admin Starter Dashboard.

### Not Required for Starter

- Full patient portal.
- Full clinical record.
- Billing system.
- Multi-clinic support.
- Advanced Clinical Intelligence.

## Professional Release

### Customer Promise

DentalOperix Professional supports the daily operation of a clinic with patients, doctors, appointment lifecycle, and improved data quality.

### Required Modules

- Patient Management.
- Doctor Dashboard.
- Appointment Lifecycle.
- Attendance/no-show classification.
- Patient contact and billing profiles.

## Business Release

### Customer Promise

DentalOperix Business helps clinic owners understand and improve conversion, attendance, and operational performance.

### Required Modules

- Advanced analytics.
- No-show analytics.
- Funnel analytics.
- Clinical Intelligence MVP.
- AI-assisted follow-up suggestions.

## Enterprise Release

### Customer Promise

DentalOperix Enterprise supports multi-location and high-scale dental organizations.

### Required Modules

- Multi-clinic structure.
- Multi-doctor capacity planning.
- Advanced roles and permissions.
- Advanced audit/compliance.
- External integrations.
