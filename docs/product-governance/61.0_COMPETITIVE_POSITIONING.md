\# 61.0 Competitive Positioning

Status: ACTIVE

Owner: Product Governance

Last Updated: 2026-06-20

\## Purpose

This document defines how DentalOperix positions itself against alternative solutions currently used by dental clinics.

The goal is not to criticize competitors but to identify market opportunities, differentiation points, and product priorities.

\---

\# Market Reality

Most dental clinics operate using one of four approaches:

1\. Manual tools (Excel, Google Sheets, Calendar).

2\. Appointment-focused systems.

3\. Dental management systems.

4\. Enterprise clinic management platforms.

DentalOperix aims to evolve beyond appointment management into an Intelligent Operating System for Dental Clinics.
