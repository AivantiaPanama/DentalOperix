Title: RBAC Lead Protection Policy

Status: Approved

Date: 2026-06-21

Decision:
No role may create, physically delete, replace, or bypass Leads through RBAC.

Rules:

- `lead.create` is denied for all user roles.
- Lead creation remains exclusively tied to `BookingDialog -> processDentalLead -> /api/leads/create`.
- `lead.delete.physical` is denied for all roles.
- Visible removal of a Lead must be modeled as a logical status change only.
- `lead.owner.reassign` is restricted to Administrator.

Architecture Compatibility:
This decision preserves `Leads = Source of Truth` and does not reopen Program 57.x.

---

## DEC-013

Title: User Lifecycle Policy

Status: Approved

Date: 2026-06-21

Decision:
User lifecycle is governed through create, update, deactivate, and reactivate operations. Physical user deletion is prohibited for all roles.

Rules:

- Only Administrator can create users.
- Only Administrator can assign roles.
- Only Administrator can deactivate or reactivate users.
- `user.delete.physical` is denied for all roles.

Rationale:
This preserves auditability, historical associations, and future compliance readiness.

---

## DEC-014

Title: Appointment Lifecycle Protection

Status: Approved

Date: 2026-06-21

Decision:
Appointment physical deletion is prohibited. Cancellation is the terminal lifecycle operation.

Rules:

- `appointment.delete.physical` is denied for all roles.
- `appointment.cancel` is the approved terminal operation.
- Cancelled appointments remain in the system as historical records with cancelled status.

Rationale:
This preserves operational traceability across Booking, Calendar, Gmail, ICS, and future reporting.

---

Title: Implementation Authorization for 61.1 Users + RBAC + Dashboard Routing

Status: Approved

Date: 2026-06-22
