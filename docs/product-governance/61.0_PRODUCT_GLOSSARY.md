\# 61.0 Product Glossary



Status: ACTIVE



Owner: Product Governance



Last Updated: 2026-06-20



\## Purpose



This document defines the official terminology used throughout DentalOperix.



All documentation, AI systems, dashboards, reports, and implementations should use these definitions consistently.



\---



\# Core Business Terms



\## Lead



A person who has expressed interest in receiving services from the clinic.



Examples:



\* Website form submission

\* WhatsApp inquiry

\* Phone inquiry

\* Marketing campaign response



Governance Rule:



Leads are the official commercial source of truth.



\---



\## Appointment



A scheduled interaction between a patient and the clinic.



An appointment may be:



\* Requested

\* Scheduled

\* Confirmed

\* Completed

\* Cancelled

\* No Show



\---



\## Patient



A person registered by the clinic for clinical or operational management.



A patient may originate from:



\* A Lead

\* A Manual Registration

\* An Imported Record



A patient can have:



\* Multiple phone numbers

\* Multiple email addresses

\* Billing information

\* Clinical information



\---



\# Appointment Lifecycle



\## Requested



Appointment has been requested but not yet confirmed.



\---



\## Scheduled



Appointment has been assigned a date and time.



\---



\## Confirmed



Patient has acknowledged or accepted the appointment.



\---



\## Completed



Appointment occurred successfully.



\---



\## Cancelled



Appointment was cancelled before completion.



\---



\## No Show



Patient did not attend the appointment.



Governance Note:



No Show tracking is critical for operational analytics.



\---



\# User Domain



\## User



A person with authenticated access to DentalOperix.



Every user belongs to exactly one role.



\---



\## Patient User



Portal user associated with a patient profile.



Primary capabilities:



\* View appointments

\* Receive notifications

\* Update personal information



\---



\## Assistant User



Operational clinic staff member.



Primary capabilities:



\* Manage appointments

\* Manage leads

\* Contact patients

\* Confirm appointments



\---



\## Doctor User



Clinical professional.



Primary capabilities:



\* View assigned appointments

\* Access patient information

\* Record treatment information



\---



\## Administrator User



System administrator.



Primary capabilities:



\* User management

\* Role management

\* Operational reporting

\* Configuration



\---



\# Security Domain



\## Authentication



Process used to verify user identity.



\---



\## Authorization



Process used to determine what a user may access.



\---



\## RBAC



Role-Based Access Control.



Permissions are assigned through roles rather than individual users.



\---



\# Analytics Domain



\## Lead Conversion Rate



Percentage of leads that become appointments.



Formula:



Appointments Created / Leads Generated



\---



\## Appointment Conversion Rate



Percentage of scheduled appointments that are completed.



Formula:



Completed Appointments / Scheduled Appointments



\---



\## Attendance Rate



Percentage of appointments attended by patients.



Formula:



Completed Appointments / Scheduled Appointments



\---



\## No Show Rate



Percentage of appointments where patients do not attend.



Formula:



No Shows / Scheduled Appointments



\---



\## Patient Retention



Percentage of patients who return for additional services.



\---



\# Clinical Intelligence Domain



\## Clinical Intelligence



AI-assisted insights generated from operational and clinical data.



Examples:



\* Attendance predictions

\* No Show predictions

\* Operational recommendations

\* Capacity optimization
