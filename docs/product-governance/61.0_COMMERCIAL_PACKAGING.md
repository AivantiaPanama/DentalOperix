\# 61.0 Commercial Packaging



Status: ACTIVE



Owner: Product Governance



Last Updated: 2026-06-20



\## Purpose



This document defines the commercial packaging strategy for DentalOperix.



DentalOperix may be sold before the complete Dental Operating System vision is finished.



Commercial releases are designed to deliver value incrementally while maintaining compatibility with the certified architecture and long-term roadmap.



\---



\# DentalOperix Starter



\## Target Customer



Small and medium dental clinics.



Typical profile:



\* 1 to 5 doctors

\* 1 to 3 assistants

\* No formal CRM

\* Appointment management challenges

\* Lead tracking challenges



\## Primary Value Proposition



Convert more leads into appointments and manage daily operations from a single platform.



\## Included Modules



\### Commercial Operations



\* Lead Capture

\* Lead Management

\* Appointment Booking

\* Appointment Confirmation



\### Integrations



\* Google Calendar

\* Gmail Notifications

\* ICS Calendar Invitations



\### Persistence



\* Certified Supabase Persistence



\### Operations



\* Assistant Dashboard

\* Admin Starter Dashboard



\### Security



\* Users

\* Basic RBAC



\## Not Included



\* Clinical Records

\* Treatment Management

\* Advanced Analytics

\* Multi-clinic Operations



\## Dependencies



Required:



\* 61.1 Users \& RBAC

\* 61.2 Assistant Dashboard



\## Commercial Readiness



Current Status:



IN\_PROGRESS



Estimated Readiness:



4 to 8 weeks



Business Value:



Very High
