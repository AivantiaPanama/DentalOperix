## Executive Summary

PR-61.2-04A implemented a read-only Lead Detail view inside the Assistant / Front Desk Workspace.

The implementation allowed Front Desk users to open a Lead from the certified Lead Queue and inspect Lead details without creating, editing, updating status, adding notes, deleting, assigning, scheduling, or writing to persistence.

Status update behavior was intentionally excluded from PR-61.2-04A and later introduced separately through PR-61.2-04B Lead Status Management after explicit Architecture Review and approval.
