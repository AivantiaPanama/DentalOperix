- Lead Queue tests updated to reflect new controlled appointment workflow capability.

- Leads remain Source of Truth.
- Appointment is a complementary operational scheduling domain.
- Provider assignment is appointment-level only.
- This PR does not model Doctor <-> Patient or Lead <-> Patient relationships.
