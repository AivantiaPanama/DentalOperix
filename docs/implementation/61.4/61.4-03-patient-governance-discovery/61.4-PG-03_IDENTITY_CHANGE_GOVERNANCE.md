# 61.4-PG-03 IDENTITY CHANGE GOVERNANCE

Phase: 61.4  
Track: Patient Governance Discovery  
Status: COMPLETE

## Objective

Define how changes to Patient identity should be governed.

## Change Levels

### Level 1: Minor Corrections

Examples:

- Typo in name
- Typo in email

Requirement: Audit required.

### Level 2: Relevant Corrections

Examples:

- Primary phone change
- Primary email change

Requirement: Audit required, stronger review recommended.

### Level 3: Critical Corrections

Examples:

- Date of birth
- Core identity attributes
- Matching-relevant attributes

Requirement: Reinforced audit and future role-based approval design.

## Restrictions

Not allowed:

- Silent identity changes
- Automatic heuristic corrections
- Mass automatic identity modifications

## Governance Principle

Every identity change must be traceable, auditable and conceptually reversible.
