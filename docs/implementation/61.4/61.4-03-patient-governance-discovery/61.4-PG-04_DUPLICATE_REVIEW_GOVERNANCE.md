# 61.4-PG-04 DUPLICATE REVIEW GOVERNANCE

Phase: 61.4  
Track: Patient Governance Discovery  
Status: COMPLETE

## Objective

Define how possible duplicate Patients should be reviewed.

## Conceptual Flow

1. Detection of possible duplicate.
2. Classification by confidence level.
3. Human review by authorized actor.
4. Review result recorded.

## Confidence Levels

- Low confidence
- Medium confidence
- High confidence

## Review Outcomes

- NOT_DUPLICATE
- REQUIRES_INVESTIGATION
- POTENTIAL_DUPLICATE_CONFIRMED

## Critical Restriction

Confirming a duplicate does not imply merge.

Detection, confirmation and consolidation are separate governance concepts.

## Prohibited

Automatic Patient Merge remains prohibited.
