# 61.4-PG-01 PATIENT GOVERNANCE MODEL

Phase: 61.4  
Track: Patient Governance Discovery  
Status: COMPLETE

## Objective

Define authority and ownership over Patient identity.

## Fundamental Principle

Patients are the Source of Truth for person identity.

No other domain may become owner of identity.

## Ownership Matrix

| Domain       | Ownership                                  |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------ |
| Leads        | Acquisition, marketing and lead lifecycle. |
| Patients     | Person identity.                           |
| Appointments | Scheduled operational events.              |

## Governed Actions

Allowed under future controlled workflows:

- Create Patient
- Update Patient identity attributes
- Correct capture errors
- Review possible duplicates

Not allowed:

- Replace Patient identity from Lead
- Replace Patient identity from Appointment
- Automatic merge
- Transfer identity ownership to another domain

## Governance Principle

Patient identity belongs to the Patient domain and can only be modified through auditable processes.
