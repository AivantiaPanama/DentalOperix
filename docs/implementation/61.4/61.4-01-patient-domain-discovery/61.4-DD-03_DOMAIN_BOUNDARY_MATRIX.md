## 4. Allowed Cross-Domain Relationships

### Lead to Patient

Allowed:

```text
Lead may create or reference Patient after identity resolution.
```

Not allowed:

```text
Lead replaced by Patient.
Lead persistence rewritten because Patient exists.
Dual write between Lead and Patient as source-of-truth replacement.
```

---

### Appointment to Patient

Allowed:

```text
Appointment may reference Patient for scheduled operational events.
```

Not allowed:

```text
Appointment becomes identity source of truth.
Appointment stores permanent identity as owned data.
Appointment lifecycle controls Patient lifecycle automatically.
```

---

### Patient to Future Domains

Allowed:

```text
Patient may be referenced by future Treatment, Billing, Insurance, Consent or Tenant domains.
```

Not allowed:

```text
Patient implements clinical EHR.
Patient calculates insurance coverage.
Patient owns billing automation.
Patient owns marketing lifecycle.
```
