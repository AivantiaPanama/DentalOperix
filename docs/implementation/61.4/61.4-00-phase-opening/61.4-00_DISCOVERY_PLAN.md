# 61.4-00 DISCOVERY PLAN

Phase: 61.4  
Status: DISCOVERY PLAN  
Date: 2026-06-23

## Objective

Discover, define and govern the Patient domain before any design or implementation activity.

## Planned Discovery Tracks

1. Patient Domain Discovery
2. Identity Discovery
3. Patient Governance Discovery
4. Patient Architecture Discovery
5. Discovery Closure and Certification

## Success Criteria

Discovery is complete when:

- Patient domain responsibilities are defined.
- Patient lifecycle is documented.
- Domain boundaries are documented.
- Identity signals and collisions are analyzed.
- Governance and auditability rules are defined.
- Patient architecture blueprint is documented.
- Closure and certification reviews are complete.

## Constraints

No implementation, schema changes, API changes, UI changes or protected component modifications are authorized during this phase.
