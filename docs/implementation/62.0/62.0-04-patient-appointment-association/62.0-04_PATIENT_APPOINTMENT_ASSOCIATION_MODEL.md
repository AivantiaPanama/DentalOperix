# 62.0-04 Patient Appointment Association Model

## Objective

Define how Patients and Appointments relate without making Patient the scheduling owner.

## Cardinality

- One Patient may be associated with many Appointments.
- One Appointment may be associated with zero or one Patient.

## Relationship States

Unlinked, Candidate, Identity Pending, Confirmed, Corrected, Rejected, Escalated.

## Rules

- Appointment may exist without Patient.
- Patient association does not transfer appointment ownership.
- Appointment evidence may trigger identity review, but cannot overwrite Patient identity.
- Corrections require actor, timestamp, reason, previous association, new association, and review state.

## Protected Boundary

Calendar and Gmail behavior are not modified by this design.
