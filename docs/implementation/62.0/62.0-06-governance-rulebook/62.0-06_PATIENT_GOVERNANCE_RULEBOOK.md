# 62.0-06 Patient Governance Rulebook

## Objective

Formalize who can decide, what can be decided, what cannot be decided, what must be reviewed, and what must be audited.

## Roles

| Role | Can | Cannot |
|---|---|---|
| System | Detect candidates, calculate confidence, generate evidence and alerts. | Merge Patients, approve identity decisions, replace Leads. |
| Authorized Reviewer | Review evidence, accept/reject associations, escalate cases. | Override restricted cases. |
| Governance Administrator | Resolve escalations, approve sensitive corrections, apply restrictions. | Mutate audit history or bypass RBAC. |

## Restricted Actions

Automatic Patient Merge, Lead Replacement, New Lead Source of Truth, Persistence Re-Architecture, RBAC Bypass, identity decisions without audit, silent identity correction.

## Compliance Checklist

- Leads remain Source of Truth.
- Patients remain Source of Truth.
- Appointments remain Source of Truth.
- Evidence, confidence, review, and audit exist.
- No RBAC bypass or silent correction exists.
