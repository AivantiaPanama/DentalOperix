# 62.0-03 Lead Patient Relationship Model

## Objective

Define how Leads and Patients relate without Lead Replacement.

## Cardinality

- One Patient may be associated with many Leads.
- One Lead may be associated with zero or one Patient.

## Relationship States

Unlinked, Candidate, Under Review, Confirmed, Rejected, Escalated.

## Rules

- A Lead may exist without a Patient.
- Lead evidence may create a Patient candidate.
- Confirmed association requires evidence, confidence, review, and audit.
- Lead lifecycle remains Lead-owned.

## Prohibited Behaviors

Patient overwrites Lead, Lead becomes Patient automatically, Lead deletion after Patient creation, Lead status moved into Patient, marketing data copied into Patient as owner, automatic Patient merge.
