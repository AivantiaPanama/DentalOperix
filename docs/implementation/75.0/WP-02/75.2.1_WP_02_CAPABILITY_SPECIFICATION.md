# 75.2.1 — WP-02 Capability Specification

## Capability

**Clinical Notes Foundation**

DentalOperix gains the capability to register, consult, update and visualize clinical narrative information through Clinical Notes.

## Actor context

### Primary actor

- Doctor / Healthcare Professional.

### Secondary actors

- Clinical specialists.
- Administration with authorized read access.

### Indirect beneficiaries

- Patient.
- Clinical continuity processes.
- Future clinical analytics.

## Capability ecosystem

Clinical Notes enables the first real clinical information flow inside Clinical Records.

It will later support:

- Clinical Assessment.
- Diagnosis.
- Treatment Planning.
- Dental Charting.
- Clinical Evolution.

## Scope

Included:

- Clinical Notes as clinical narrative entries.
- Chronological clinical history foundation.
- Clinical author and audit metadata.

Excluded:

- Diagnoses.
- Treatment plans.
- Odontogram.
- Attachments.
- Prescriptions.
- Clinical AI.
