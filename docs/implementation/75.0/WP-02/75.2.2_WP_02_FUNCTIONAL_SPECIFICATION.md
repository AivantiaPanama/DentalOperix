# 75.2.2 — WP-02 Functional Specification

## Purpose

Implement the first functional capability of the Clinical Records domain by allowing authorized healthcare professionals to create and maintain Clinical Notes.

## Functional cases

### UC-01 Create Clinical Note

The doctor records clinical narrative information associated with a patient.

### UC-02 Update Clinical Note

The doctor amends an existing Clinical Note while preserving audit metadata.

### UC-03 View Clinical History

The doctor views the chronological set of Clinical Notes belonging to a patient.

### UC-04 View Clinical Note Detail

The doctor opens a specific Clinical Note to review its narrative content.

## Business rules

- Every Clinical Note belongs to one patient.
- Every Clinical Note belongs to one Clinical Record.
- Every Clinical Note has an authorized healthcare professional as creator.
- Clinical information is owned exclusively by Clinical Records.
- Appointment association is optional and referential only.
- Patient identity is not modified by Clinical Records.
- Leads remains isolated.

## Out of scope

- Structured diagnosis.
- Treatment planning.
- Dental charting.
- Clinical attachments.
- Prescriptions.
- Clinical AI.
