# 62.1-06 Persistence Impact and Option Analysis

## Objective

Evaluate Patient persistence options without implementation.

## Rejected Options

- Embed Patient inside Lead.
- Lead stores full Patient state.
- Patient owns Lead collection.

## Accepted Principle

Independent Patient persistence is the only compatible conceptual option. Lead owns Lead persistence, Patient owns Patient persistence, Appointment owns Appointment persistence.

## Association Principle

Lead Patient and Appointment Patient relationships are associations, not ownership transfers.

## Impact

No impact to LeadPersistencePort, LeadPersistenceProvider, RelationalLeadPersistenceAdapter, or protected components.
