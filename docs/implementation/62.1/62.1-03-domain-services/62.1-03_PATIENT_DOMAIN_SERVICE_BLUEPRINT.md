# 62.1-03 Patient Domain Service Blueprint

## Objective

Define conceptual Patient domain services without implementation.

## Proposed Services

- PatientIdentityService: manages Patient identity state.
- IdentityEvidenceService: captures and classifies evidence.
- IdentityConfidenceService: evaluates confidence, not decisions.
- IdentityReviewService: governs human review decisions.
- PatientRelationshipService: manages governed associations.
- PatientAuditService: records immutable audit events.

## Boundary Rules

No Patient service modifies lead status, lead source, lead campaign, appointment status, date/time, provider assignment, Calendar state, or Gmail state.
