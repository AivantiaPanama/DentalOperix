# 62.1-01 Canonical Patient Identifier Strategy

## Objective

Define how Patient identity can be created, referenced, associated, audited, and governed without depending on mutable attributes.

## Decision

PatientId is the canonical technical identifier.

## Rejected Identifiers

Phone, email, name + birth date, LeadId, AppointmentId.

## Rules

- PatientId never changes.
- PatientId is not derived from business data.
- PatientId is not reused.
- PatientId does not decide identity; governance decides identity.

## Open Decisions

Physical format, generation strategy, external exposure, indexing strategy.
