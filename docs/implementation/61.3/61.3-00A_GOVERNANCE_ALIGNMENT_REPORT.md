## 3. Governance Rules Adopted

### Execution Continuity Rule

Adopted from 61.2 Governance Retrospective Candidates.

Rule:

```text
If objective, scope and approval exist, and no business/architecture/security decision is pending, execute the next planned activity and produce the next deliverable without requesting redundant confirmation.
```

Exceptions:

- Architectural decision pending
- Business rule ambiguity
- Security or compliance concern
- Scope change request

### Domain Discovery Gate

Adopted after successful use in Appointment and Patient domain discovery.

Rule:

```text
Before implementing a new or materially changed domain, perform domain discovery, capture ADRs, identify watch items, and obtain explicit approval of the domain boundary.
```
