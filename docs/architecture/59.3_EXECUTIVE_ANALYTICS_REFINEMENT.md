## Objective

59.3 refines Executive Analytics from a score-and-ranking view into an action-oriented executive decision layer.

The phase adds:

- executive health status
- risk level
- opportunity level
- executive narrative
- primary focus
- priority actions

These outputs are derived exclusively from existing `RevenueSnapshotV1` and `RevenueForecastSnapshot` data.

## Architectural Position

```text
RevenueSnapshotV1
  -> RevenueForecastSnapshot
  -> ExecutiveAnalyticsSnapshot 59.3-v1
  -> ExecutiveAnalyticsPanel
```

Executive Analytics remains read-only. It does not write lead data, does not persist alternate state, and does not create a new source of truth.
