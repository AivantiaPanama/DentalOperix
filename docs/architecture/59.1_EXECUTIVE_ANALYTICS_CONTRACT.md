## Score Semantics

Executive scores use a 0-100 scale:

```text
excellent >= 80
healthy   >= 60
watch     >= 40
critical  <  40
```

Scores are derived indicators for prioritization. They are not financial records and do not represent certified revenue recognition.

## 59.3 Contract Extension

59.3 extends the original 59.1 contract without changing the architectural position of Executive Analytics.

New fields:

```text
ExecutiveAnalyticsSnapshot.interpretation
ExecutiveAnalyticsSnapshot.priorityActions
```

`interpretation` contains:

- `healthStatus`
- `riskLevel`
- `opportunityLevel`
- `narrative`
- `primaryFocus`

`priorityActions` contains action-oriented executive recommendations derived from Revenue Intelligence and Revenue Forecasting snapshots.

The extension remains read-only and does not change persistence, source of truth, lead creation, or any certified 57.x decision.
