## Purpose

58.5 adds deterministic forecasting on top of Revenue Intelligence. The objective is to move from descriptive analytics to simple forward-looking indicators without adding persistence, model training, AI dependencies, or alternate sources of truth.

Forecasting v1 answers:

- expected estimated revenue over 30 days
- projected pipeline value over 30 days
- expected appointments over 7 and 30 days
- conversion, attendance, and lead-volume trend direction
- executive risks and alerts
- confidence level and limitations


The endpoint is read-only and protected by the same `reports:read` permission boundary used by Revenue Intelligence.

## Calculation Model

Forecasting v1 is deterministic and intentionally conservative.

It uses:

```text
RevenueSnapshotV1
  -> trend data points
  -> lead velocity
  -> lead-to-appointment rate
  -> estimated pipeline
  -> expected estimated revenue
```

It does not use:

- machine learning
- external AI
- new persistence
- background jobs
- lead mutation
- financial/payment source-of-truth assumptions

## Revenue Semantics

All revenue values remain estimated.

Revenue Forecasting v1 must not be interpreted as actual collected revenue because the 58.2 Revenue Intelligence contract exposes:

```text
revenueType = estimated
```

This phase does not introduce real revenue recognition, payment reconciliation, invoicing, or treatment acceptance accounting.
