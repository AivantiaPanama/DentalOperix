## Official v1 Formulas

### Lead to Appointment Rate

```text
leadToAppointmentRate = (agendada + completada) / totalLeads
```

Implemented through the existing CRM metric formula `calculateConversionRate`.

### Appointment to Attendance Rate

```text
appointmentToAttendanceRate = completada / (agendada + completada)
```

Implemented through the existing CRM metric formula `calculateAttendanceRate`.

### Lead to Attendance Rate

```text
leadToAttendanceRate = completada / totalLeads
```

### Estimated Pipeline Value

```text
estimatedPipelineValue = sum(SERVICE_ESTIMATED_VALUE[treatment])
```

Cancelled and no-show leads are excluded by the existing CRM metric formula.

### Expected Revenue

```text
expectedRevenue = estimatedPipelineValue * leadToAppointmentRate
```

This is explicitly estimated revenue, not realized revenue.

The contract exposes:

```text
revenueType = estimated
```

## Status Normalization

The contract normalizes lead statuses internally for analytics purposes only.

Known statuses:

- `nuevo`
- `agendada`
- `completada`
- `cancelada`
- `no asistió`

Compatibility normalization:

- `no asistio` -> `no asistió`

Unknown or missing statuses are reported through the quality section and are not written back to the source record.

## Data Quality Counters

The v1 contract reports:

- `missingSource`
- `missingService`

These counters are diagnostic and read-only.
