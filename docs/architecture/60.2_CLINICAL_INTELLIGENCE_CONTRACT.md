## Known Limitations

- Clinical Intelligence v1 depends on clinical read-model completeness.
- It does not calculate real revenue or treatment profitability.
- It does not mutate treatment plans, stages, outcomes, patients, leads, calendar or communications.
- It does not provide diagnosis or treatment recommendations.
