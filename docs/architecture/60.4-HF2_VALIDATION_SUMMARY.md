## Implemented Behavior

- Lead persistence remains through `LeadPersistenceProvider` and the relational adapter.
- Google Calendar event now includes patient email and clinic notification email as attendees, deduplicated.
- Calendar event creation requests `sendUpdates: "all"`.
- Gmail sends patient confirmation and clinic operational notification independently.
- `processDentalLead` reports `patientEmailSent` and `clinicEmailSent` separately.
- Partial notification failure does not roll back the saved lead.
- `.env` examples now include `CLINIC_NOTIFICATION_EMAIL`.
- New living scope document added to preserve project continuity across chats.
