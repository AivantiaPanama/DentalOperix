## Lessons Learned

- Separating identity from acquisition was correct.
- Defining Sources of Truth early reduced risk.
- Pre-implementation governance avoided costly rework.
- Explicit component protection reduced operational risk.
- The greatest threat was organizational confusion between review approval and implementation authorization.
