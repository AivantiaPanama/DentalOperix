### GRC-005 - Execution Continuity Rule

Category: ADD

Origin:

Identified during 61.2-06A Appointment Domain Discovery.

Problem:

Once an objective, scope and approval exist, the process may continue

requesting confirmations instead of executing the next planned activity.

Proposed Rule:

If:

\- Objective approved

\- Scope approved

\- No pending business decision

Then:

\- Execute next activity

\- Produce next deliverable

\- Do not request redundant confirmation.

Exceptions:

\- Architectural decision pending

\- Business rule ambiguity

\- Security or compliance concern

\- Scope change request

Expected Benefit:

Reduce governance friction while preserving architectural control.

Initial signal:

Positive. During 61.2-06A multiple approval cycles occurred after scope and objectives were already approved. Executing the next planned activity directly would likely have reduced friction without reducing governance quality.
