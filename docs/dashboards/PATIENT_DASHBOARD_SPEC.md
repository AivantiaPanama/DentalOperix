# Patient Dashboard Specification

Status: DRAFT
Owner: Product / UX Governance
Last updated: 2026-06-20

## Goal

Help patients manage their relationship with the clinic.

## Primary Questions

- When is my next appointment?
- Can I add it to my calendar?
- What service did I book?
- How can I update my contact or billing data?
- What is my future treatment or visit history?

## Core Components

1. Next Appointment Card
2. Calendar / ICS Actions
3. Contact Information
4. Billing Profile Summary
5. Visit History Placeholder
6. Clinic Contact Actions

## Governance

Patient self-service actions require Users/RBAC before implementation.
