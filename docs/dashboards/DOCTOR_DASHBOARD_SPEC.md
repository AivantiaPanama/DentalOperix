# Doctor Dashboard Specification

Status: DRAFT
Owner: Product / UX Governance
Last updated: 2026-06-20

## Goal

Help doctors view their agenda and patient context.

## Primary Questions

- Which patients am I seeing today?
- What service or treatment is expected?
- What patient context is relevant?
- What follow-up is needed?

## Core Components

1. My Schedule Today
2. Next Patient Card
3. Patient Context Snapshot
4. Treatment Notes Placeholder
5. Follow-up Reminders

## Governance

Clinical notes and treatment records are future scope and require separate clinical data governance.
