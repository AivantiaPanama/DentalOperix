\# RBAC 61.1 Foundation

Official Roles:

\- Patient

\- Assistant

\- Doctor

\- Administrator

Key Rules:

\- lead.create = prohibited

\- lead.delete = prohibited

\- user.delete = prohibited

Only Administrator may:

\- assign roles

\- deactivate users

\- reactivate users

\- reassign lead ownership

Open Architecture Questions:

\- Doctor ↔ Patient Assignment

\- Lead ↔ Patient Relationship

\- Retention Policy

\- Role Assignment Workflow

Do not invent solutions for open architecture questions.
